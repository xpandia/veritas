The Problem

Justice has a price tag.
Most people can't afford it.

The current arbitration system is designed for corporations with unlimited budgets, not for the people who need it most.

Prohibitive Cost

Filing fees, arbitrator compensation, legal representation, administrative costs. A simple contract dispute can drain a small business dry before a decision is ever reached.

$91,000+ average cost per commercial arbitration case

Agonizing Delay

Scheduling conflicts, document discovery, hearing preparation, deliberation periods. The process that was meant to be faster than litigation now mirrors its worst traits.

12-18 mo average time to resolution
The Solution

Replace arbitrators with
AI consensus validators.

Veritas runs on GenLayer, the only blockchain where smart contracts can reason, analyze evidence, and reach fair verdicts.

AI-Powered Reasoning

Multiple independent AI validators analyze contracts, evidence, and applicable legal frameworks to form reasoned judgments.

Decentralized Consensus

No single arbitrator. No single point of bias. GenLayer's consensus mechanism ensures multiple AI agents independently agree on the outcome.

On-Chain Enforcement

Verdicts are binding and self-executing. Escrow releases automatically. No appeals to a slow court system.

How It Works

Three steps. That's it.

From dispute to resolution in minutes. No lawyers, no waiting rooms, no uncertainty.

1

File Your Dispute

Submit the contract, evidence, and your claim. Everything is recorded immutably on-chain. Both parties deposit escrow.

2

AI Deliberates

GenLayer's AI validators independently analyze the case, consider precedent and contract terms, then reach consensus on a verdict.

3

Resolved

A binding verdict is issued. Smart contract escrow releases funds to the prevailing party. Transparent, final, fair.

Trust & Security

Trust the process,
not a person.

Every safeguard is mathematical, not institutional.

Immutable Evidence

All submissions are hashed and stored on-chain. No party can alter evidence after filing.

Multi-Validator Consensus

Multiple AI agents reason independently. Collusion is mathematically impossible when validators can't coordinate.

Escrow-Protected

Funds are locked in smart contract escrow before deliberation begins. The verdict triggers automatic, trustless release.

Fully Transparent

Every step of the reasoning process is recorded on-chain. Audit the logic, verify the verdict, trust the outcome.
